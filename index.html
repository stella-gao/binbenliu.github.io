I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at New Jersey Institute of Technology. My advisor is Professor .

I am broadly interested in deep learning, machine learning, data science, and biostatistics. In particular, I recently focus on:

  • Recommender systems (effective and scalable methods to model user behavior and choices, and apply them for emerging applications such as mobile apps and location-based services)
  • Location-based services (point-of-interest recommendation, spatio-temporal analytics)
  • Mobile apps (app recommendation, privacy and ethics in mobile app ecosystem)
  • User attribute inference from digital records of human behavior
