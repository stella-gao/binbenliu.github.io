Bin Liu

 

I am a PhD student in the Department of Management Science and Information Systems at Rutgers Business School, Rutgers University. My advisor is Professor Hui Xiong.

I am broadly interested in data mining, data science, and business analytics. In particular, I recently focus on:

  • Recommender systems (effective and scalable methods to model user behavior and choices, and apply them for emerging applications such as mobile apps and location-based services)
  • Location-based services (point-of-interest recommendation, spatio-temporal analytics)
  • Mobile apps (app recommendation, privacy and ethics in mobile apps)
  • Demographic inference and profiling from social media and user generated contents

Contact: binben.liu@rutgers.edu
